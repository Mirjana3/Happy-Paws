About Our Shelter

Happy Paws Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing abandoned and homeless animals. The mission is to provide love, care, and a safe home until every pet finds a family.

Our team of 15 volunteers works daily to ensure every animal gets a second chance. We've successfully rehomed over 500 pets with 95% adoption success rate.
